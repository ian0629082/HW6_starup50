# Startup Profit AI Simulator Video Design

## Palette
- Paper: #F7F0DD
- Ink: #16212A
- Teal: #00A8A8
- Golden: #F4C84A
- Violet: #8B5CF6
- Coral: #F05D5E
- Leaf: #65B96A

## Typography
- Headline voice: "Arial", 900
- Body voice: "Arial", 600
- Data voice: "Consolas", 700

## Motion
- Professional and calm, with page-turn transitions.
- Use restrained kinetic accents: slide tilt, light sweep, soft zoom, and clean caption reveals.
- Avoid jump cuts and avoid cropping the original slides.

## Composition
- Format: 1080 x 1920 portrait.
- Original 16:9 slides appear as full preserved cards inside the portrait frame.
- Captions sit in the lower third and never cover the main slide content.
